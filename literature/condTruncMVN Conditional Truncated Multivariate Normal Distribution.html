The goal of condTruncMVN is to find densities, probabilities, and samples from a conditional truncated multivariate normal distribution. Suppose that Z = (X,Y) is from a fully-joint multivariate normal distribution of dimension n with mean ŒºŒº and covariance matrix sigma ( Œ£Œ£ ) truncated between lower and upper. This package computes the conditional truncated multivariate normal distribution of Y|X. The conditional distribution follows a truncated multivariate normal [1]. Specifically, the functions are arranged such that
